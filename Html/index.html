What is DNA?

DNA (deoxyribonucleic acid) contains your genetic code, and it's
unique. You get a copy of your genes from each of your parents.
The places where your genes are different are called genetic
variants.

Sometimes you end up with a variation in your genetic code that
is associated with a health issue. Fortunately, you can change
the expression of many of your genes.
